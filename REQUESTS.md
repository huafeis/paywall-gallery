# App Requests

Open Paywall Gallery will add 50 new iOS subscription apps every week.

Open an Issue if you want us to add a specific app: [request an app](https://github.com/paywallpro/paywall-gallery/issues/new?template=app-request.yml).

## What to include

Useful requests include:

- App name
- App Store URL
- Country or region
- Why this app is interesting
- What you want to learn from its paywall or onboarding flow

## Good request signals

- Strong subscription revenue
- Interesting paywall structure
- Notable free trial or pricing strategy
- Strong onboarding flow before the paywall
- Important competitor in a specific category
- Recent paywall, pricing, or subscription flow change

For broken screenshots or metadata problems, open a data problem Issue: [report a problem](https://github.com/paywallpro/paywall-gallery/issues/new?template=data-problem.yml).
