# Paywall Pattern Taxonomy

This document defines the main paywall patterns used in the Open Paywall Gallery.

## Free Trial Soft Paywall

A paywall that presents premium value and lets users start with a free trial.

Common traits:

- Trial CTA
- Benefit-led copy
- Reduced upfront risk
- Often paired with annual plan anchoring

## Hard Paywall

A paywall that blocks access until the user subscribes.

Common traits:

- No meaningful free path
- Strong CTA
- High conversion pressure
- Often used after onboarding or personalization

## Multi-Offer Paywall

A paywall that presents multiple subscription plans, tiers, or offer types.

Common traits:

- Multiple plan cards
- Monthly, annual, or weekly options
- Best-value badges
- Feature or benefit comparison

## Promotional Paywall

A paywall that highlights a limited-time discount or special offer.

Common traits:

- Discount messaging
- Urgency
- Countdown or one-time offer language
- Often shown after paywall close or during reactivation

## Onboarding Paywall

A paywall shown during or immediately after onboarding.

Common traits:

- Personalized setup
- Result preview
- Progress completion
- Subscription offer shown before full product access
