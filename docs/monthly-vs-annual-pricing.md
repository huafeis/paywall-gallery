# Monthly vs Annual Pricing

A collection of paywalls that present monthly and annual subscription options.

## Why this pattern matters

Monthly plans reduce entry friction. Annual plans can increase cash flow and retention when positioned as the best-value option.

The strongest paywalls usually make the comparison easy to understand.

## What to study

- Which plan is selected by default
- Whether savings are shown
- How annual value is framed
- How monthly price is used as an anchor
- Whether weekly pricing is used to make the offer feel smaller
