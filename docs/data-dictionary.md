# Data Dictionary

This document explains the core fields used in the Open Paywall Gallery.

## App name

The public App Store name of the app.

## Category

The primary App Store category used to group the app.

Examples:

- Entertainment
- Productivity
- Photo & Video
- Lifestyle
- Business
- Music

## Estimated MRR

Estimated monthly recurring revenue.

This value is used as a directional monetization signal. It should be treated as an estimate rather than an official financial number.

## Paywall pattern

A simplified classification of the paywall structure.

Common patterns include:

- Free Trial Soft Paywall
- Hard Paywall
- Multi-Offer Paywall
- Promotional Paywall
- Onboarding Paywall
- Monthly vs Annual Pricing Paywall

## Pricing model

A summary of how the subscription plans are structured.

Examples:

- Monthly only
- Monthly and annual
- Weekly and annual
- Free trial with annual plan
- Multiple subscription tiers

## Screenshots

Representative paywall screenshots included in the public repo.

The public repo may only include a limited number of compressed preview images. Full screenshot history is available in PaywallPro.

## Key takeaways

Human-readable notes summarizing what builders can learn from the paywall.

A link to explore the complete app record inside PaywallPro.
