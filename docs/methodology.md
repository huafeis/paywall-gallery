# Methodology

Open Paywall Gallery is a public research gallery built by PaywallPro.

The goal is to help app builders, product managers, growth teams, and designers study real iOS subscription paywalls from successful apps.

## How apps are selected

Apps may be selected based on:

- Subscription relevance
- Category importance
- Estimated revenue scale
- Product popularity
- Interesting paywall structure
- Pricing or offer design
- Usefulness as a reference for builders

## How screenshots are used

This repository includes representative preview screenshots for research, commentary, and educational purposes.

The public repository only includes a compact preview layer. Full screenshot sets, historical versions, and onboarding flows are available in PaywallPro.

## How paywall patterns are classified

Paywall patterns are simplified to make the gallery easier to browse.

A paywall may include multiple patterns, but each public page usually highlights the most useful primary pattern.

## How monetization signals should be interpreted

Revenue, MRR, ranking, and other monetization fields should be treated as directional estimates or research signals.

They are useful for comparison, prioritization, and pattern discovery, but they should not be treated as official financial statements.

## Public data boundary

This repository is designed as a public preview and research gallery.

PaywallPro contains the deeper subscription intelligence layer, including full screenshot history, onboarding flows, historical version changes, pricing experiments, and advanced analysis.
