# Free Trial Paywalls

A collection of paywalls that use free trials to reduce subscription friction.

## Why this pattern matters

Free trials can make a paid subscription feel easier to start. They are especially useful when the app needs users to experience premium value before paying.

## What to study

- Trial length
- Trial messaging
- CTA copy
- Annual plan anchoring
- Cancellation reassurance
- Benefit presentation before price
